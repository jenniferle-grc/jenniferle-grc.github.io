Welcome to Matcha Muse, your haven for all things matcha. Located in the heart of San Diego, CA, we bring you an inviting space to sip, savor, and connect over the timeless art of matcha.

At Matcha Muse, we’re passionate about delivering an authentic and modern matcha experience. From velvety lattes to artfully crafted desserts, our menu is a celebration of matcha’s versatility and vibrant flavor. Every item is crafted with the finest premium matcha sourced directly from Japan, ensuring a rich, smooth taste in every bite and sip.

But we don’t stop at serving delicious drinks and treats. For those who want to bring the magic of matcha into their own homes, we offer our exclusive Matcha Muse Set—a curated collection of tools and matcha powder designed for creating your perfect cup.

Whether you’re a long-time matcha lover or new to its charm, Matcha Muse is here to inspire your matcha journey. Come for the flavor, stay for the vibe, and leave feeling refreshed and uplifted.
